About Us

Postcard Travel Club is a global membership community of mindful travellers interested in immersive travel experiences that promote powerful self-growth and personal transformation. Through our website we hope to inspire you on your journey of life, with local stories of community, culture, history, food, nature and wildlife. From the Wilderness of Botswana to the Beyuls of Nepal, While our community is currently online, we believe in the value of human-to-human connections. Starting soon, we will be opening City Chapters for our members to organise local meet-ups, pop-up events, weekend-gertaways, and more. We are also developing concepts around Reward Cards, Fundraisers and Sponsored Giveaways. These are exciting times and we thank you for your support and encouragement.
